Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 29, 2024

ActiveReader ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using ActiveReader, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in using Apple Sign In or Google Sign In, we collect:

  • User ID: A unique identifier provided by Apple or Google
  • Email Address: Your email address (if provided by the sign-in provider)
  • Name: Your display name (if provided by the sign-in provider)

This information is used solely for app functionality, including account management and service delivery.

1.2 Reading Data

We collect and store the following data related to your use of the App:

  • Books: PDF and EPUB files you import into the App
  • Highlights: Text you highlight while reading
  • Flashcards: AI-generated flashcards created from your highlights
  • Quizzes: Quiz questions and answers generated from your reading
  • Reading Progress: Your progress through books

This data is stored locally on your device and in your user account in our cloud database.

1.3 Usage Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data, including:

  • App launches and session duration
  • Feature usage (flashcard generation, quiz creation, etc.)
  • Error and crash reports
  • Performance metrics

This data is aggregated and anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally.

1.4 Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription, we collect:

  • Subscription status and purchase history
  • Trial period information
  • Transaction identifiers (processed through Apple's App Store)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: To enable core app features, including flashcard generation, quiz creation, and reading progress tracking
  • To Process AI Requests: To generate flashcards and quizzes from your highlighted text using AI services (OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google Gemini)
  • To Manage Subscriptions: To track subscription status, trial periods, and usage limits
  • To Improve the App: To analyze usage patterns and improve app functionality
  • To Fix Issues: To identify and fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues
  • To Communicate: To send you important updates about the App (if you opt in)

3. Data Storage

3.1 Local Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and file storage:

  • Books (PDF/EPUB files)
  • Highlights
  • Flashcards
  • Quizzes
  • Reading progress

This data remains on your device and is not automatically synced to other devices.

3.2 Cloud Storage

The following data is stored in our cloud database (Firebase Firestore):

  • User account information (User ID, email, name)
  • Subscription status and history
  • Trial period information
  • Usage statistics (flashcard generation count, etc.)

This data is used to manage your account and subscription across devices.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect or process your information:

4.1 Authentication Services

  • Apple Sign In: Provided by Apple Inc. See Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign In: Provided by Google LLC. See Google's Privacy Policy

4.2 Firebase Services (Google)

We use Google Firebase for:

  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics and crash reporting
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reporting and error tracking
  • Firebase Firestore: Cloud database for user account data

Firebase is provided by Google LLC. See Firebase Privacy Information and Google's Privacy Policy.

4.3 AI Services

To generate flashcards and quizzes, we send your highlighted text to one of the following AI providers:

  • OpenAI: For GPT-based flashcard generation. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic: For Claude-based flashcard generation. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini: For Gemini-based flashcard generation. See Google's Privacy Policy

Important: When you generate a flashcard, the text you highlight is sent to the selected AI provider to generate the flashcard content. This text is processed by the AI service and is subject to their privacy policies. We do not store the highlighted text on our servers after processing.

4.4 App Store

Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. See Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how Apple handles payment information.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party service providers (Firebase, AI providers) who assist us in operating the App, subject to their privacy policies
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to valid legal requests
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users)

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • User authentication is handled securely through Apple and Google's authentication systems
  • Cloud data is stored in Firebase Firestore with appropriate access controls
  • Local device data is protected by iOS security features

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Correction: Update or correct your account information

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided at the end of this policy.

7.2 Data Portability

You can export your data (books, highlights, flashcards) from the App at any time. Your data is stored locally on your device and can be accessed through the App.

7.3 Opt-Out

You can opt out of analytics tracking by disabling analytics in your device settings. However, this may limit our ability to improve the App.

7.4 Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time through the App settings. This will:

  • Delete your account information from our cloud database
  • Remove your subscription information
  • Delete all local data from your device

Note: Some data may be retained in backups for a limited period as required by law or for business purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

ActiveReader is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

Third-party services (Firebase, AI providers) may process your data in various locations worldwide, subject to their respective privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notifying you through the App (for significant changes)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when posted on this page.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.
